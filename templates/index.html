WORKFLOW

The following is the expert system workflow for diagnosing your skin disease. You can consult with us what type of service is suitable for your health.

Search DiagnoHealth

First of all, you have to search for diagnosehealth.com in the web browser of your choice.

See Workflow

On the website page, please see or read the workflow of this system.

Consultation

In this stage, users will be asked to upload images or photos of the skin disease they are experiencing.

Tracking

At this tracking stage, the image or photo uploaded by the user will be trained by the system and predicted what skin disease the user is suffering from.

Details info

This stage is the result of an image or photo that has been uploaded which states the user's skin disease.

Emergency care

In this final stage the user will be given a solution or way to cure the disease they are experiencing.
